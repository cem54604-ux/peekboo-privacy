👀 PeekBoo - Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Introduction

PeekBoo ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application handles your information.

2. Camera Usage

PeekBoo uses your device's front camera solely to detect faces in real-time. This is the core functionality of the app — detecting when someone is looking at your screen.

  • Camera data is processed entirely on your device
  • No images or video are recorded or stored
  • No camera data is transmitted to any server
  • Camera is only active while the app is in use

3. Data We Collect

We collect minimal data:

  • Face detection results: Only the count of detected faces (a number), processed on-device only
  • App preferences: Your selected sound pack and settings, stored locally on your device
  • Peek count: Number of times a curious eye was detected, stored locally only

4. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do NOT collect photos or videos
  • We do NOT collect biometric data or face recognition data
  • We do NOT collect personal identification information
  • We do NOT share any data with third parties
  • We do NOT use advertising networks

5. On-Device Processing

All face detection is performed locally on your device using Google ML Kit. No data leaves your device.

6. Children's Privacy

PeekBoo is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the date at the top of this page.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: peekbooapp@gmail.com
